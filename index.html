Frequently Asked Questions

Does DockSpace affect my system performance?

No, DockSpace is designed to be lightweight and uses minimal system resources. It won't slow down your Mac.

Can I customize the position of the widgets?

Yes, you can arrange widgets in any order you prefer and choose which ones to display.

Will this work with my external display?

DockSpace works with any display configuration where your dock is visible.

How do I add new widgets?

Simply open the DockSpace preferences and select from the available widgets to add them to your dock area.
