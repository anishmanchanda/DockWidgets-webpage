Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to grant a lot of permissions?

No, DockWidgets only needs permissions to run scripts to control music playing in Spotify or Apple Music.

Is the installation process complicated?

No, the installation process is straightforward. Just download and unzip the file and move the app to your Applications folder.


Since we don't have Apple approval yet, you may need to bypass Gatekeeper by right-clicking the app and selecting "Open." and choose "Open Anyway" in privacy settings.

Will the widgets block my apps?

No, the widgets are designed to be within the Dock's height hence not block any content from any app

How do I change the location for weather?

Simply open the DockWidgets preferences by right clicking and type in any location.
